Recent Projects

The Pocket Pantry

My biggest project yet which is available in the AppStore! I was the app engineer in my team and used Flutter and Hive database to create The Pocket Pantry! It was made to help people find food banks and soup kichens easier.

Since this type of information is scattared across many government websites, we decided to provife users to this information in one cohesive place and use filters such as boroughs, hours, special requirements, and even if it's mobile or kosher and more! This is to make sure People can get the relevant information they need without having to scower for it! We also added the requirements or special notes for each place to ensure people can get the resources they require!

HalalApp

My other flutter project which I would like to publish as well! I used Flutter to create an app that helps users find halal restaurants in NYC.

It is tailored to the needs of the Muslim community in New York City since searching on google doesn't always provide with accurate information. A problem I face myself is finding out that a resturant I googled is closed by the time I get there so it is helpful to know and even plan where to go based on what is open and what I prefer. I used Firebase to implement CRUD operations to store and receive data of restaurants in NYC which included pictures, hours, boroughs, and more details.
